Academic Coaching

Our students are located from Michigan to California, aged Pre-K to adults.
The focus of our academic coaching is English and Language Arts. However, we offer expertise in ACT and SAT preparation in both the English and Science sections of the tests, AP test, college entry assistance, English as a Second Language, teaching early reading and writing, and business and career communications.
Our students are successful in increasing school grades, developing language skills, raising ACT and SAT scores, getting acceptance to prestigious schools, and winning scholarships and writing awards.
We work one-on-one, in-person, and remotely with students, and focus on areas that need improvement. We always go the extra mile and encourage students to do the same. In the process, we have fun and enjoy learning.

About Me

Janet Lawless, M.A., Director, has over twenty years of teaching experience, instructing college English and private school classes, and is a published author of both articles and creative work. She is an expert in K-12 and college ELA curriculum, ACT, SAT, English and Literature AP, and college application essays. She has a passion for teaching, and the growth of her academic coaching services is one of her proudest.
